Try Greetably Now!

Category: Photo sharing

Greetably makes it easy to post images and greetings to your Facebook page.
  1. Click on an image to share on your page.
  2. Write your message in the text box.
  3. Click on the "Share Image to Facebook" button.>
  4. If you are not logged into Facebook, it will prompt you to log in now.
  5. You are done! Your image and message is now shared.
